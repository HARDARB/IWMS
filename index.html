Waste Recycle at a glance!

  • Collection: Recyclable materials are generated by a consumer or business and then collected by a private hauler or government entity.
  • Processing: Recyclables are sorted, cleaned, and reprocessed into new materials for manufacturing new products.
  • Remanufacturing: After processing, recyclables are made into new products at a recycling plant or similar facility..
